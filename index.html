WELCOME

What are we doing!!!


We strive hard to meet the needs of agents and corporations, travelling for both business and leisure. To further enhance its competitiveness, the company has embarked on various expansion plans to strengthen its position in the tourism industry.

We firmly believe in a creative working atmosphere, as this would encourage professional minds to produce the best solutions and value-added services to our customers as fast and efficiently as possible.

We believe in not only giving stress free holidays but also plan the holidays in the best possible way, as the hotel and resort we select are amongst the best in their respective categories in terms of comfort and pricing. We also intend to offer niche destinations to travelers who wish to venture into new territories.
